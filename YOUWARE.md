# Giftly.in — Digital Gift Cards for India

## Project Status

- **Project Type**: React + TypeScript + Vite + Tailwind CSS
- **Entry Point**: `src/main.tsx`
- **Build System**: Vite 7.0.0

## What is Giftly.in?

Giftly.in is a modern digital gift card marketplace designed for Indian consumers. Users can purchase digital gift cards for local restaurants, cafes, salons, and shops across India, with personalized greeting cards and instant delivery via WhatsApp or email.

## Key Features

- **Digital Gift Cards** — For local Indian restaurants, cafes, salons, and shops
- **Personalised Greeting Cards** — Free customisable e-cards with every order
- **Instant Delivery** — Via WhatsApp & Email
- **Currency** — Indian Rupees (₹), range ₹50 to ₹50,000
- **Local Marketplace** — Focus on Indian neighbourhood businesses
- **Brand Gift Cards** — Top Indian brands (Starbucks, Swiggy, Zomato, etc.)

## Design System

- **Primary Color**: Saffron Orange (#FF6B35) — `brand-500`
- **Accent Color**: Purple (#7C3AED) — `accent-600`
- **Heading Font**: Playfair Display (serif)
- **Body Font**: DM Sans (sans-serif)
- **Card Style**: Glass-morphism with subtle hover lift effects
- **Animations**: fadeInUp, slideIn, float, pulseSoft

## Page Sections

1. **Navbar** — Fixed top navigation with search, links, and CTA
2. **Hero** — Gradient background with search bar and trust badges
3. **Browse by Occasion** — 12 category cards (Birthday, Diwali, Wedding, etc.)
4. **Season's Best Gifts** — 6 curated gift card collections
5. **Top Brands** — 8 popular Indian brand cards
6. **Local Favourites** — 6 local restaurant/shop cards with ratings
7. **How It Works** — 3-step process (Pick, Personalize, Send)
8. **Greeting Cards** — 6 customizable card designs
9. **WhatsApp & Email Delivery** — Feature highlight section
10. **CTA** — Create your own gift card
11. **Footer** — Links, social media, legal

## Tech Stack

- React 18 + TypeScript
- Vite 7 (build tool)
- Tailwind CSS 3.4
- Lucide React (icons)
- Framer Motion (available for future animations)
- Zustand (available for state management)
